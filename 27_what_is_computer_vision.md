# Computer Vision

Computer Vision is the field of AI that enables computers 
to "see" and interpret visual information from images or 
videos - recognizing objects, people, scenes, and understanding 
what's happening in them.

## Simple analogy:
Humans look at a photo and instantly know what's in it - 
a dog, a car, a person walking. Computer vision teaches 
machines to do the same thing, turning pixels into 
meaningful understanding.

## Real world examples:
- Face recognition on phones
- Self driving cars detecting pedestrians and signs
- Medical imaging detecting tumors
- Sports analytics (detecting players and tracking movement)
