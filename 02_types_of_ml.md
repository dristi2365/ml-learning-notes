# Types of Machine Learning

## 1. Supervised Learning
You give the model labeled data — the answers are already provided.

Example: Show it 1000 cat photos labeled "cat" and 1000 dog 
photos labeled "dog". It learns the difference and predicts 
the label for new images.

Called "supervised" because you provide correct answers during training.

Two types:
- Classification — predicting a category (spam/not spam)
- Regression — predicting a number (house price)

## 2. Unsupervised Learning
You give the model data without any labels. It finds patterns on its own.

Example: Give it 10,000 customer purchase records with no labels.
It groups customers into clusters on its own.

## 3. Reinforcement Learning
The model learns by trial and error — like training a dog with 
rewards and punishments.

Example: AI playing chess — wins get reward points, losses get 
penalized. Over millions of games it figures out winning moves.

## Summary
- Supervised — learns from labeled examples
- Unsupervised — finds patterns without labels
- Reinforcement — learns through rewards and punishments
