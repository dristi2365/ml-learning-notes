# Pose Estimation

Pose estimation detects the positions of key body parts - 
shoulders, elbows, wrists, hips, knees - essentially 
creating a "skeleton" of a person in an image or video.

## How it helps:
Instead of just knowing "where a person is" (a bounding box), 
pose estimation tells you HOW their body is positioned - 
arm angles, posture, movement direction.

## Tools:
MediaPipe (by Google) is a popular tool that does real time 
pose estimation, detecting 33 body landmarks.

## Real world uses:
- Sports analytics - analyzing technique and form
- Fitness apps - counting reps, checking posture
- Sign language recognition
- Gaming and motion capture
- Physical therapy - tracking rehabilitation progress
